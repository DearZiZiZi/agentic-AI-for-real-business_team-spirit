# HappyCake US — Agent Ordering Guide

HappyCake is a bakery in Sugar Land, TX. Traditional cakes, made by hand. This document helps AI agents order on behalf of customers.

## Products

Products come from the MCP catalog. Key items:
- Cake "Honey" — slice ($8.50) or whole 1.2 kg ($55)
- Cake "Pistachio Roll" — slice ($9.50)
- Custom birthday cake — from $95 (requires owner consultation)
- Office dessert box — $120 (serves 8-10)

Always check live catalog via GET /api/agents/catalog for current prices and availability.

## How to Order

1. GET /api/agents/catalog — browse available products with live prices
2. GET /api/agents/availability?date=YYYY-MM-DD — check kitchen capacity
3. GET /api/agents/policies — read ordering policies and allergen info
4. GET /api/agents/products/{slug} — get details for a specific product
5. POST /api/agents/order-intent — place an order

## Order Intent Format

POST /api/agents/order-intent
Content-Type: application/json
Idempotency-Key: <unique-key>

{
  "product": "whole honey cake",
  "quantity": 1,
  "pickup_date": "2026-05-16",
  "pickup_time": "15:00",
  "customer_name": "Jane Doe",
  "notes": "For a birthday party, 10 guests"
}

## Constraints

- Standard items: 24 hours advance notice
- Custom cakes: 5-7 business days, requires owner consultation
- Kitchen capacity is limited — always check availability before ordering
- Allergens: kitchen handles nuts, dairy, eggs, wheat, soy, gluten
- All prices in USD cents in the API
