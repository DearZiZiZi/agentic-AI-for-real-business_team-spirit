# HappyCake US — Policies

## Ordering
- Orders placed at least 48 hours in advance for standard items
- Custom cakes require 5-7 business days notice
- Wedding cakes require a consultation and 2-week minimum lead time

## Pickup
- Pickup from our Sugar Land, TX location
- Please bring your order confirmation
- Orders held for 24 hours after scheduled pickup time

## Pricing
- All prices listed include tax
- Custom cake pricing provided after consultation
- Deposit of 50% required for custom orders over $100

## Cancellations
- Standard orders: cancel up to 24 hours before pickup for full refund
- Custom orders: deposit is non-refundable once production begins
- Contact us immediately if you need to make changes

## Allergens
- Our kitchen handles: nuts, dairy, eggs, wheat, soy, and gluten
- We cannot guarantee an allergen-free environment
- Please inform us of any allergies when ordering
